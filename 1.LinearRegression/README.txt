Linear Regression with Batch Gradient DescentYou will train your model using a real world dataset. The dataset contains a collection of real estate listings in San Luis Obispo county.https://wiki.csc.calpoly.edu/datasets/wiki/Houses (https://wiki.csc.calpoly.edu/datasets/wiki/Houses)
It contains the following fields:1. MLS: Multiple listing service number for the house (unique ID).2. Price: the most recent listing price of the house (in dollars).3. Price/SQ.ft: price of the house per square foot.The model predict the housing price given the square footage of the house.
